IRIS DATA SET SUMMARY

The iris dataset is a collection of data on the morphological variation of three species of iris flowers.

The dataset has five features: petal length, petal width, sepal length, sepal width, and species type3.

The dataset can be used for discriminant analysis, as well as unsupervised classification or clustering.

The objective of the dataset is to quantify and compare the differences among the three species.
